Cracker Barrel Hash Brown Casserole

Ingedients-

2 LBs frozen hashbrowns

1/2 cup melted margarine or butter

1 (10 1/4 oz) can of Cream of Chicken soup

1 pint Sour cream

1/2 cup onion, peeled & chopped

2 cups grated or shredded cheddar cheese

1 tsp salt

1/4 tsp pepper

Directions-

1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF

2. Generously spray an 11x14 in. pan with cooking spray

3. Mix all of the ingredients together (See below for cheese)

(You can vary the amounts of Salt, Pepper, and sour cream to taste)

4. For a more gooey center, sprinkle some cheese in with the mix

5. Cover the top of the mix with the cheese, adding more if desired

6. Seriously, mess that shit up with cheese, you'll thank me later

7. Bake in oven for 45 minutes, or until top of mix is golden brown

8. Serve hot, serves about 12
