Shel Silverstein

![Shel Silverstein](./images/shel.png)

Shel Silverstein (1930-1999), was a popular childrens cartoonist, poet, and author. Famous for many notable works such as "Falling up" and "Where the sidewalk ends", Silverstein wrote and published over 20 story/poetry books, and was so dedicated to his work that he would insist on choosing what kind of paper that the books would be printed on, stating that the feel and texture of the paper had a lot to do with the quality of the book.
